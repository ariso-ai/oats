# Security Policy

oats records meetings and, in Local mode, processes audio, transcripts, and
notes entirely on your machine. We take the privacy and integrity of that data
seriously and appreciate reports that help us keep it safe.

## Supported versions

oats auto-updates, and we only support the **latest released version**. Please
reproduce any issue on the most recent release (see the
[Releases page](https://github.com/ariso-ai/oats/releases/latest)) before reporting.

## Reporting a vulnerability

**Please do not open a public issue for security vulnerabilities.**

Report privately through GitHub's
[**Report a vulnerability**](https://github.com/ariso-ai/oats/security/advisories/new)
flow (Security tab → Advisories). If you can't use that, email
**security@ariso.ai**.

Please include:

- A description of the issue and its potential impact.
- Steps to reproduce, or a proof of concept.
- The oats version and macOS version you tested on.
- Which backend was involved (Ariso cloud or Local / on-device), if relevant.

## What to expect

- We'll acknowledge your report within **3 business days**.
- We'll work with you to confirm the issue and determine its severity.
- We'll keep you updated on remediation and coordinate disclosure timing.
- With your permission, we're happy to credit you when the fix ships.

Thank you for helping keep oats and its users safe.
